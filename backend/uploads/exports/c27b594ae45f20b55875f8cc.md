### **Project Description**
This project entails the detailing, fabrication, and erection of a 180-ton, three-story structural steel frame. The building's lateral stability is achieved through a moment-resisting frame system, providing significant architectural freedom by eliminating the need for diagonal bracing. All connections are designed to be fully rigid or semi-rigid, with design, materials, and workmanship specified in accordance with AISC 360-22. The completed steel structure will serve as the primary load-bearing system for a commercial or institutional facility.

*   **Severity:** **Critical**
*   **Implication:** Major coordination failure. Either the HVAC route must be significantly altered (impacting headroom and other services), or the structural girder requires a large, reinforced opening, which must be designed and verified by the Structural Engineer of Record. This is a significant cost and schedule risk.

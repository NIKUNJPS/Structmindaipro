A standard SSPC 3-coat system (e.g., inorganic zinc primer, epoxy mid-coat, polyurethane topcoat) provides excellent corrosion protection but can contain high levels of Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs).

*   **Standard System VOCs:** Can range from 250 to 400 g/L. VOCs contribute to smog formation and poor air quality.
*   **Low-Emission Recommendations:**
    1.  **Specify Low-VOC Formulations:** Mandate coatings with VOC content **<100 g/L**. Most major manufacturers offer high-performance, low-VOC equivalents.
    2.  **Consider High-Solids Coatings:** These systems have less solvent to evaporate, reducing VOC emissions and often requiring fewer coats.
    3.  **Shop Application:** Prioritize coating application in a controlled shop environment rather than on-site to manage emissions and improve quality control.

### 5. Connection Summary
A review of the defined connection types indicates a mix of standard shear and more complex moment connections.

*   **Shear Connections:** Primarily single-plate shear tabs. Assumed to be per AISC *Steel Construction Manual*, 15th Ed., Part 10. Connection design will be delegated to fabricator's engineer.
*   **Moment Connections:** Bolted Extended End-Plates. Design will be delegated and must conform to AISC Design Guide 4, *Extended End-Plate Moment Connections*. RFI is required to confirm if seismic provisions (AISC 341) apply.
*   **Bracing Connections:** Standard gusset plates for HSS braces. Work points and eccentricities must be clarified with EOR.
*   **Bolting:** Standard bolts will be 3/4" dia. ASTM F3125 Gr. A325-TC. Moment connection bolts will be 1" dia. ASTM F3125 Gr. F1853-TC (A490). All faying surfaces in slip-critical connections to be prepared as Class A.
*   **Welding:** All shop welding to conform to AWS D1.1. Assume E70XX electrodes. All CJP welds at moment connections will require NDT (UT).

### 6. Critical Notes, General Notes & Specifications Cross-Check
The following items require immediate attention during model setup and drawing creation:
*   **AESS Specification (AISC 303-22):** Note G-001 must define the exact locations, columns, and elevation extents for AESS Category 2. All welds in AESS zones must be ground smooth and flush. All piece marks and erection marks must be placed in concealed locations.
*   **Paint Specification:** An SSPC system is noted. Assume SSPC-SP6 Commercial Blast Cleaning with one shop coat of universal primer at 3.0-5.0 mils DFT. Color to be confirmed. AESS members will require a higher-quality finish system, to be specified by the architect.
*   **Camber:** Beams with lengths exceeding 30 ft are expected to require camber. A camber schedule or note indicating the basis for calculation (e.g., 80% of dead load deflection) is required from the EOR.
*   **Survey Control:** A site survey control point must be provided by the General Contractor to which all anchor bolt and steel geometry will be referenced.

*   **Coordination:** The model will be exported to IFC format for coordination with MEP and architectural models. Clash detection will be run prior to issuing drawings for fabrication.

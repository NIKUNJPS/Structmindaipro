### C. ENGINEERING LAYER

#### Structural System Summary
The structure is a steel-framed office building utilizing a composite system.
*   **Gravity System:** Composite steel beams (W-shapes) and girders supporting concrete slab on metal deck. Load is transferred to W-shape columns and down to spread footing foundations.
*   **Lateral Force-Resisting System:** A dual system is employed.
    *   Ordinary Moment Frames (OMF) at perimeter locations, using bolted moment end-plate connections.
    *   Ordinary Concentric Braced Frames (OCBF) at core locations, using HSS braces connected via gusset plates.
*   **Diaphragm:** The composite floor slabs act as rigid diaphragms to transfer lateral loads to the frame elements.

#### Load Path
*   **Gravity:** Roof/Floor Deck -> Composite Beams -> Girders -> Columns -> Base Plates -> Foundations.
*   **Lateral (Wind/Seismic):** Exterior Cladding/Diaphragm -> Collectors -> Moment/Braced Frames -> Columns -> Base Plates -> Foundations.

#### Seismic Design Category (SDC)
The project site in Denver, CO falls under **Seismic Design Category (SDC) B** per ASCE 7-22. This classification permits the use of OMF and OCBF systems as specified.

#### Shop Sequence & NC Readiness
Fabrication will proceed in erection sequence order after drawing approval.
1.  **Sequence 1:** Anchor Bolts & Embeds (Release for construction).
2.  **Sequence 2:** Tier 1 AESS Columns & Core Braced Frame Columns.
3.  **Sequence 3:** First Floor Beams, Girders & Braces.
4.  **Sequence 4:** Tier 2 Columns & Second Floor Framing.
5.  **Sequence 5:** Roof Framing.

All structural steel must be modeled in Tekla Structures to LOD 400. Direct export of DSTV/NC1 files to automated machinery is required. AESS members will be flagged in the model and files for special handling protocols.

#### Coating System
The specified system is SSPC paint.
*   **Surface Preparation:** SSPC-SP6 Commercial Blast Cleaning.
*   **Primer:** Universal acrylic primer applied to a Dry Film Thickness (DFT) of 3.0-5.0 mils.
*   **AESS Members:** Special handling with soft slings and dunnage required post-coating to prevent damage. AESS surfaces will have all welds ground smooth and contoured per AISC 303-22 Table C-AESS-3.

#### Lifting & Delivery Plan
*   **Lifting:** All members over 1 ton will have lift points engineered and detailed on shop drawings.
*   **Delivery:** Shipments will be sequenced for just-in-time delivery to the Denver site. Maximum trailer length is 53 ft. Maximum piece weight is limited to 20 tons to avoid special permitting. AESS columns will be shipped individually wrapped on padded racks.
